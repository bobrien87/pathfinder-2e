You have an eye for angles and can find shots that others believe are impossible. When using [[Ricochet Shot]], you can bounce your bullet off up to two solid surfaces within your weapon's first range increment instead of just one. In addition, a creature is [[Off Guard]] the first time you attack it with a Ricochet Shot during an encounter.
