Your sneak attack slices through the threads binding magic to a target. Make a Strike against an [[Off Guard]] creature. If your Strike deals sneak attack damage, you attempt to Class check{counteract} a single spell of your choice active on the target. Your counteract rank is equal to half your level (rounded up), and your counteract check modifier is equal to your class DC – 10.
