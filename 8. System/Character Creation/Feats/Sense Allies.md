Like many humans raised in a close-knit community, you have always been strongly attuned to the presence of others. Willing allies that you are aware of within 60 feet that would otherwise be [[Undetected]] by you are instead [[Hidden]] from you. The flat check for you to target willing allies within 60 feet that are hidden from you is flat check instead of 11.
