###  Antigravity Support

This vault includes built-in support from Google Antigravity (AI). This is entirely optional and you can ignore it if you don't want to use it.

#### Rules
Rules are used to ensure that anything created using AI in the vault maintains a consistent format, look and feel, and works with the plugins that are installed.
